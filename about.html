Our Mission

The University of Michigan Supermileage Team is a student-run, multidisciplinary engineering design team that designs and fabricates a hyper-fuel-efficient, single-occupant vehicle. Our team competes in the Shell Eco-Marathon Americas, an international competition that brings together universities and high schools from across the continents. We strive to push the boundaries of automotive innovation, promoting sustainable transportation and providing an unparalleled hands-on engineering experience for our members.
