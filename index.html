Ventus

A deep dive into our prototype class vehicle. The Ventus is the culmination of years of aerodynamic research and lightweight material science. Its carbon fiber monocoque chassis provides exceptional rigidity at a fraction of the weight of traditional frames.

×

Aurum

Exploring the Aurum, our urban concept car. Designed to meet practical road-going requirements like headlights and turn signals, the Aurum still achieves incredible fuel economy. Its design philosophy balances real-world usability with extreme efficiency.

×

Ignis

Ignis represents the future of our team's engineering. This next-generation vehicle explores alternative powertrains and cutting-edge manufacturing techniques, serving as our primary R&D platform for the next decade of competition.
